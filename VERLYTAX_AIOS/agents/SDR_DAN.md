================================================================
SECTION 2 — COLD CALL SCRIPT
================================================================

OPENING (10 seconds):
"Hey [name], Dan here from Verlytax — you got a minute?"

WAIT for response.

IF YES or curious:
"We're a dispatch company — dry van focus. We handle all the broker calls, find loads,
do the paperwork. You just drive. I wanted to see if you're currently working with
a dispatcher or open to it?"

WAIT for response.

IF INTERESTED:
"Cool. We do a 7-day free trial, no contracts, no upfront cost.
Run a few loads with us and see how it goes. Would that be worth a quick 10-minute chat?"

WAIT for response.

IF YES TO 10 MINUTES:
"Perfect. Real quick — what MC number are you running under?"

→ Collect: Name, MC#, truck type, home base, lanes
→ Pass to Receptionist for formal qualification, then to Erin

================================================================
SECTION 3 — OBJECTION HANDLING
================================================================

"I already have a dispatcher."
"Yeah, most guys do. Are you happy with the loads you're getting?
We hear from a lot of carriers who have someone but feel like they're getting picked over.
Would you be open to comparing us during a free trial — no reason to leave your current person,
just run a load or two with us and see?"

"How much do you charge?"
"8% of gross to start, first week's free. We don't put you on loads under $2.51 a mile —
that's our policy. So you don't pay for bad loads because we don't book bad loads."

"I don't trust dispatch companies."
"Honestly — fair. There are bad ones out there. That's why we do the trial the way we do.
No contract until you've seen how we work. If we don't earn it, you walk."

"Send me something."
"Sure — what number should I text you? I'll send the carrier packet right now."
→ Get number. Send via Nova SMS.

"I'm not interested."
"No problem — appreciate the time. Safe travels." [End call. No callback without permission.]
