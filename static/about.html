Frequently Asked Questions

What does ClearRoute actually do for me? ▼
We handle everything that isn't driving. That means load booking, rate negotiation, broker screening, invoicing, collections, dispute escalation, BOL management, and compliance tracking. You run your truck. We run the business side.
What does it cost? ▼
Days 1–7: Free. No credit card needed. After your trial, it's 8% of your gross revenue for Months 1–4. If you stay with us beyond Month 4, new carriers go to 10% — but original carriers (OG carriers) stay at 8% for life. Minimum $100/week applies once you're billing. That's the whole fee structure.
How do you find loads? ▼
Our AI dispatcher Erin works load boards and broker relationships to find freight that meets our Iron Rules: $2.51+ RPM, max 50-mile deadhead (25%), max 48,000 lbs, no Florida, and only brokers with a clean history in our system. We don't take just any load — we take the right load.
What if a broker doesn't pay? ▼
Erin handles the dispute. Any billing issue under $200 with clear documentation gets resolved on our end — you don't have to make a single call. Anything over $500, or any dispute that's unresolved after 48 hours, gets escalated directly to our CEO. Blocked brokers are added to our permanent block list so they never touch our network again.
Do I need to be enrolled in NDS? ▼
Yes. NDS (National Drug and Alcohol Testing Program) enrollment is required before we dispatch your first load. It's $100/year and the carrier pays it directly. This is non-negotiable — it protects you, your authority, and the shippers we work with.
Can I talk to a real person? ▼
Yes. Erin is our AI dispatcher but there is always a human in the loop. When a situation requires a judgment call — a big money decision, a carrier dispute, a retention issue — our CEO steps in directly. You're not going to be stuck in a chatbot loop when something actually matters.
What types of equipment do you dispatch? ▼
We currently dispatch dry van and flatbed in Phase 1 USA operations. Max weight 48,000 lbs. We verify all equipment and authority types at enrollment. If you run something different, contact us — we'll tell you straight whether we can work together.
What happens after my free trial ends? ▼
We'll send you a clear summary of what we did during your trial — loads booked, revenue generated, issues handled. Then we'll walk you through your fee structure and get you set up on Stripe auto-pay on Monday billing. No pressure. If the trial didn't show value, you walk away with no obligation and no invoice.
