About ClearRoute Dispatch

ClearRoute Dispatch is an AI-powered freight dispatch operation managing a vetted network of owner-operators and small fleet carriers across the continental USA (Phase 1). We handle all carrier-side communication, compliance documentation, load coordination, and billing — so your team deals with one professional point of contact, not a dozen individual drivers.

Every carrier in our network is personally onboarded, FMCSA-verified, Clearinghouse-enrolled, and operating under our Iron Standards — a set of non-negotiable rules enforced at the system level. When you book with a ClearRoute carrier, you're not guessing at compliance. We've already done it for you.

Our dispatcher, Erin, is your direct contact for all load communication. She confirms details, manages pick-up and delivery windows, handles check calls, and escalates the moment anything needs human attention. You get professionalism on every load — not just the easy ones.

Our Carrier Standards

Every carrier dispatched through ClearRoute has passed all of the following requirements before their first load is accepted.

🏛️
FMCSA Authority — Active & Verified
Minimum 180 days active. Verified through the live FMCSA portal — not paper certificates or email screenshots.
✅
Satisfactory Safety Rating Only
We do not dispatch Conditional or Unsatisfactory carriers. Period. No exceptions for any reason.
💊
FMCSA Clearinghouse Enrolled
Every carrier is enrolled in the FMCSA Drug & Alcohol Clearinghouse. Failed tests result in immediate and permanent block from our network.
🛡️
Current COI on File
Certificate of Insurance on file with ClearRoute. Minimum $1M auto liability. We track expiration dates and alert before lapse.
🏋️
Max Load Weight 48,000 lbs
All loads are confirmed within legal weight limits before acceptance. No overweight loads dispatched.
🧪
NDS Drug Testing Program
All ClearRoute carriers are enrolled in the National Drug and Alcohol Testing Program before their first dispatch.

Broker FAQ

What lanes do your carriers run?
We currently operate USA Phase 1 — continental US lanes excluding Florida (pickup or delivery). Our carrier base is primarily Midwest, Southeast (non-FL), Mid-Atlantic, and Heartland lanes. Contact us with your specific origin and destination for immediate availability.
What equipment types are available?
Phase 1 carriers run dry van and flatbed. Max weight 48,000 lbs. We do not currently dispatch refrigerated, heavy haul, or oversized loads.
Can we add ClearRoute to our carrier list?
Yes. We'll send you a carrier packet with all required documentation — MC, USDOT, COI, W-9, and carrier agreement. Turn time for a complete packet is same business day.
What happens if there's a problem on a load?
Contact Erin immediately. Any issue on a load gets escalated internally within 1 hour. If a load requires a significant decision — claims, delays, substitution — our operations team responds directly. We do not leave you without a point of contact.
How do we report a problem carrier or issue?
Contact us at any time. If a carrier in our network creates a problem — service failure, conduct issue, documentation miss — we want to know immediately. Problem carriers are reviewed and removed from our dispatch network without exception.
