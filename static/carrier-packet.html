Who We Are

ClearRoute Dispatch is an AI-native freight dispatch operation built from the ground up to protect carriers — not exploit them. We handle the full business side of your operation: load booking, rate negotiation, broker vetting, invoicing, collections, and dispute resolution.

Our dispatcher, Erin, is available around the clock. Our standards are non-negotiable. Our fee structure is transparent. We built ClearRoute for owner-operators and small fleets who are tired of being handed bad loads, ripped off by slow-pay brokers, and left to deal with compliance on their own.

You're good at driving. We're good at everything else. That's the whole model.

What We Do For You

  • Load booking — We source loads from verified brokers across major load boards, filtered to your lanes, equipment, and our minimum rate requirements.
  • Rate negotiation — Erin negotiates on your behalf. We never accept below $2.51 RPM. When rates come in low, we counter. When brokers won't move, we walk.
  • Broker vetting — Every broker is cross-checked against our blocked broker database before a load is offered. Bad actors don't get a second chance in our network.
  • Invoicing & collections — We generate and send all invoices. We chase payment so you don't have to. Late pay triggers escalation within 24 hours.
  • Dispute handling — Billing disputes under $200 with clear documentation are resolved by our team, not on your time. Larger disputes escalate to our CEO immediately.
  • BOL management — We never release the Bill of Lading before delivery is confirmed. Your leverage is protected on every load.
  • Compliance monitoring — We track your COI expiration, NDS enrollment status, and FMCSA authority. If something's about to lapse, we tell you before it's a problem.
  • HOS awareness — We don't book loads that put you in violation of hours-of-service regulations. Your logs stay clean.

What to Expect — Your First Week

  • Day 1: Erin introduces herself via SMS and confirms your first available dispatch window. We confirm your compliance docs are complete. No load is booked until everything clears.
  • Days 1–3: We learn your preferences — load timing, check-call frequency, preferred rest areas and truck stops. The more you tell us, the better we match loads to your style.
  • Days 3–5: Your first loads are offered. Erin walks you through the load details, confirms acceptance, and handles all broker communication from that moment forward.
  • Day 7: Free trial ends. We send you a summary: loads booked, miles run, revenue generated. If you're in, we transition to billing. If not, no invoice — ever.
  • Ongoing: Weekly billing every Monday. Compliance tracking automated. Disputes handled before you have to ask.

Carrier FAQ

Can I still talk to brokers directly?
Once you're dispatched through ClearRoute, all broker communication goes through Erin. This protects your rate and keeps the relationship clean. If a broker tries to contact you directly about a load we booked, refer them to us.
What if I want to take a load off my own?
You can. We dispatch you exclusively on loads we book, but you're not prohibited from running independent freight. Just keep us informed so we don't double-book a window.
What if I can't make a load window?
Call or text Erin immediately. We handle the broker communication and rebook where possible. The sooner you tell us, the better we can protect your relationship with that broker.
How does Monday billing work exactly?
Every Monday, Stripe charges 8% (or your applicable rate) of the gross revenue from loads delivered the prior week. You'll receive a billing summary via SMS from Nova before the charge runs. If a charge fails, we suspend new dispatch and notify you immediately.
What if I want to stop using ClearRoute?
30-day written notice to close out any open loads and settle final billing. After that, we cancel your Stripe profile and archive your file. No termination fee. No drama.
